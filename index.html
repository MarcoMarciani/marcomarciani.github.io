Marco Marciani

research on solid state physics and quantum computing

Postdoctoral researcher in theoretical condensed-matter physics. My current research focuses on open quantum systems, quantum algorithms and noncommutative crystals. Previously I worked on random-matrix models for quantum dots hosting Majorana states, Boltzmann transport in disordered and superconducting materials, the theory of atomic-scale nanomagnets, and optical propagation in continuous media. More generally, I am interested in technological advances in electronics, optics, nanotechnology, and quantum computation.
